Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

I've made this recipe for years, and it is a favorite at our house. It only takes about ten minutes to whip up the dough, and not much longer after that to have a warm cookie in your mouth. And I won't judge if the dough never makes it to the oven!

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks softened butter
  • 3/4 c sugar
  • 3/4 c brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 c flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1-2 tbsp corn starch
  • 1 pkg chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. If butter is cold, soften butter in the microwave.
  3. Mix butter, and both brown and regular sugar in a mixer.
  4. Add the eggs and vanilla extract, and mix.
  5. Add 1 c of flour, the baking soda and powder, salt, and corn starch. Mix.
  6. Add remaining 1 1/2 c of flour, and mix.
  7. Stir in chocolate chips.
  8. Spoon out cookies onto greased baking sheet, and bake in oven for 8 minutes.
  9. Let cool on baking sheet for a couple minutes, then move to cooling rack.
