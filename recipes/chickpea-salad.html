Chickpea Salad

Description

This is an easy recipe to throw together and keep in your fridge for easy snacks or lunches later. No cooking, it only requires chopping some veggies and dumping other ingredients from a bottle or can.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans chickpease
  • 1-2 tomatoes
  • Half a cucumber
  • 1 green pepper
  • Quarter of a red onion
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta cheese
  • Greek salad dressing

Steps

  1. Chop all the vegetables into desired size, and put in medium size bowl
  2. Rinse chickpeas, and pour into bowl
  3. Put desired amount of olives, feta cheese, and dressing
  4. Lightly stir, until ingredients are generally mixed
  5. Cover, and chill in refrigerator for at least an hour
