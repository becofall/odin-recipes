Korean Beef

Description

Quick, easy, and very tasty dinner idea! A friend posted it from the Damn Delicious Blog. It looked really good, so we adapted it a bit and it's been on the short list ever since!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 c brown sugar, packed
  • 1/2 c reduced sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp crushed red-pepper flakes, or to taste
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 c cooked ground beef
  • 4 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 tsp sesame seeds

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together brown sugar, soy sauce, sesame oil, red pepper flakes, and ginger.
  2. In a large skillet on medium heat, add ground beef, soy sauce mixture, and green onions until well combined, at least 2 minutes.
  3. Serve over rice, garnished with green onions and sesame seeds.
